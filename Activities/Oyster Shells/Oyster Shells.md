# Oyster shells
Learning anatomy of oyster shells and how they are affected by OA
- Can be paired with other oyster activities, the coral ocean acidification activity, or the reefs and waves activity 
- **Target Age Group:** K-12

## Materials 
- oyster shells
- rulers/calipers

## Activity 
1. Introductions of lab members
2. Introduce oysters and explain/discuss their shells 
	- What are they made of? (calcium carbonate)
	- Why do oysters have shells? (protects)
	- How do the shells impact the environment? (blocks wave action)
3. Introduce ocean acidification concept (basics)
	- How does this affect shell-building organisms?
4. Measure oyster shells
