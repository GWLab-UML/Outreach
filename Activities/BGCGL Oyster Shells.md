# Oyster shells
Learning anatomy of oyster shells and how they are effected by OA

## Materials 


## Activity 
●	Introductions: Each person say: name, where they are from, career stage, what they work on? What they’re interested in?
●	Introduce oysters and explain about their shells 
●	Introduce ocean acidification concept (basics)
    ●	How does this affect shell-building organisms?
●	Measure oyster shells 
●	Students learn the anatomy of an oyster’s shell and the role of the shells in protecting the oyster
