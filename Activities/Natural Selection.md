## Suggestions for next time
- Expand activity to include concepts like competition, resource availability, and niches
	- *Competition* - everyone takes food from the same pile
		- Can also get at *symbiosis* if teens end up helping each other
	- *Resource availability* - limit amount of food item(s)
	- *Niches* - multiple food types; do some predators only eat certain prey types?
- Have teens craft their own predator 
- Mimic predator feeding mechanisms on real animals
	- Fork = shark
	- Syringe = dolphin
	- Tape = octopus
	- Chip clip = crab
- Worksheet to facilitate 1) making hypotheses 2) writing down "data" 3) sketching graphs
	- Ideally this would all still be written on the whiteboard; the worksheets are for the teens to follow along and maybe have a little more stake in the game!
